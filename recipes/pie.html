Rich Chocolate Truffle Pie

Description

This luscious chocolate truffle pie is a no-bake pie with a chocolate cookie crust. It offers the best of three worlds: rich, good, and quick to make!

Ingredients

  • Chocolate chips
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Confectioners' sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cookie crumb crust

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate (with half of the cream) in the microwave in short bursts.
  2. Cool to room temperature, then stir in the sugar and vanilla.
  3. Beat the remaining cream until soft peaks form, then beat it into the chocolate mixture.
  4. Fill the pie, then allow it to set in the fridge overnight.
